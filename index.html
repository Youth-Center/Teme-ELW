What is Youth Center?

Youth Center is a non-profit and non-political organization, created by romanian students who major in different fields, such as: Foreign Languages, Social Studies, Arts (theatre, design), Mathematics and Computer Science. Our mission is to help fellow students and children advance in their studies and work towards achieving their dreams. One of our long-term goals is to create a safe environment where students can express themselves freely and get free tutoring in their studies.

PROJECTS

All of our programs relate in some way to Education, Art and Self-Development. We offer free tutoring sessions, online and offline, at our office. Youth Center provides learning materials and offers advice to students either academical or personal on time-management skills, anxiety and stress. We also work towards creating educational and financial opportunities for disadvantaged children and young adults.
