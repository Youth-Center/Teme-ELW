Projects

EXPRESS YOURSELF

We believe it's absolutely neccessary that students express themselves freely and in a safe environment. This belief shaped Express Yourself into 3 days of acting, photography and digital arts workshops.

PASS THE CLASS

Pass the Class is an educational project, focused on tutoring students and improving academic performance. Youth Center members offer free of charge online classes on subjects such as foreign languages, mathematics, literature, web design.

CRAFT A SMILE

Every Friday, our volunteers participate in an arts and crafts workshop where they create handmade jewelry and decorative objects, with the main purpose of being sold. The money are donated to local orphanages.
