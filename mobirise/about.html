Who are we?

Youth Center started out as a small project assigned to a few of our board members, when they were in the first year of college as psychology students in Cluj-Napoca. They had a month to teach highschool students something of their choosing, in the most efficient way possible. The project focused on improving the academic performance of students by strenghtening their ability to cope with stress.

The little project shaped Youth Center as we now know it. Enthusiastic students who major in different fields help other students across Romania and create opportunities for them to develop, not only academically, but also personally.

Youth Center means:

  1. Improvement We strive to help both the students who come to us and our volunteers develop themselves.
  2. Learning Everything our organization focuses on is related in some way to learning. We value knowledge of all kinds and personal development.
  3. Diversity Our programs refer to different branches of study. Also, everyone in the organization comes from different backgrounds and cultures and, therefore, we all have something to learn from each other.
  4. Connections We value teamwork and create meaningful bonds with each other through our common interests.
  5. Impact One of our long term goals is to have a lasting impact on those who seek our help and to actually make a change.
